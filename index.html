Questions

The stuff people ask before they send a bill.

How do you get paid?

One flat fee, and only if I save you money — a $129 audit for households, $299 for business accounts. If I can't beat what you're already paying, the audit is free and you owe me nothing. You keep 100% of every dollar I save you, for as long as you keep the plan. I take no carrier commissions, so my only incentive is your lowest real cost.

Do I have to switch carriers?

Never. Plenty of clients save hundreds a year by staying on their current network with a better plan and the right discounts applied. Switching is just one option on the table, not the goal.

Is my billing data safe?

Yes. I only need the statement details required to run the audit, I don't need your account password, and your information is used solely to build your report — never shared or sold.

What do you need from me to start?

One recent bill (PDF or photo) and a couple of sentences about how you use your service — travel, hotspot, number of lines. That's genuinely it for the free audit.

Does this work for small businesses?

Especially well. Business accounts accumulate the most waste — inactive lines, overage, mismatched IoT plans. The more lines you have, the bigger the leak I can usually plug.
