Questions

Good to know

How do you get paid?

One flat fee, and only if I save you money — a one-time $49.99 for households and $199 for business. Business accounts can add optional monthly maintenance, quoted to your account, to keep the savings locked in as carriers creep prices back up. If I can't beat what you're already paying, you owe me nothing — I take no carrier commissions, so my only goal is your lowest real cost.

Do I have to switch carriers?

Never. Plenty of people save just by fixing their current plan and turning on discounts they qualify for. Switching is only one option, never the goal.

Is my bill information safe?

Yes. I only need the statement details to run your estimate — never your account password — and your information is used only to build your quote. It's never shared or sold.

What do you need from me to start?

Just one recent bill (PDF or photo) and a sentence about how you use your service. That's the whole intake for your free estimate.
